Simple Omlete Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal.This classic omlette combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection, optionally filled with your choices of cheese,vegetables, or meats.

Preparation time

  • Total: Approximately 10 minutes
  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • Salt to taste
  • Pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • Optional fillings: cheese, diced vegetables, cooked meats, herbs
